Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the event dates?

A: The event will take place from October 1st to October 31st.

Q: Are there any COVID precautions in place?

A: Yes, your safety is our priority. We have implemented various measures to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all attendees. Please refer to our COVID safety page for more details.

Q: Can I take photos and videos during performances?

A: Yes, you're welcome to take photos and videos for personal use. Please be respectful of performers and other attendees.

Q: What happens if it rains? Is the event canceled?

A: The event will continue rain or shine. We advise you to bring appropriate clothing and gear.

Q: How do I find the schedule for each stage/area?

A: The schedule will be posted on our website and at the event.

Q: Are there ATMs on-site for cash withdrawals?

A: Yes, there will be ATMs available for cash withdrawals.

Q: Can I bring a backpack or bag to the event?

A: Small bags are allowed, but all bags are subject to search upon entry for security purposes.
